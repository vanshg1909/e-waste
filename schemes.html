E-Waste Management Government Schemes

Green Electronics Program

The Green Electronics Program aims to promote the recycling of electronic waste by providing incentives to businesses and individuals for proper disposal. It offers financial support and tax benefits to organizations adopting eco-friendly e-waste management practices.

E-Waste Collection Drives

The E-Waste Collection Drives initiative encourages local communities to organize and participate in e-waste collection events. Government support includes logistical assistance, awareness campaigns, and rewards for the highest contributors to e-waste recycling efforts.

Research and Innovation Grants

This scheme focuses on fostering research and innovation in the field of e-waste management. Researchers and businesses can apply for grants to develop new technologies, processes, and solutions for efficient and sustainable e-waste recycling.
